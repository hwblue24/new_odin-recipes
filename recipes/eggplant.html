Eggplant

Explosive Eggplant

Our delicious eggplant vegetarian dish will have you asking for more

We have blended together the greatest of ingredients to show you the amazing dish straight at home

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • salt
  • tomato
  • onions
  • garlic

Make it at home

  1. Cut eggplant into slices
  2. Salt eggplant slices
  3. Fry onions and garlic
  4. Add eggplant
  5. Add tomato cubed
  6. cook for 20 min low temp
